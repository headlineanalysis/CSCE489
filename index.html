Introduction

Overview and Motivation:

As of November 2016, 1440 men and women have been put to death across the United States since 1976. Over one-third of those executions have taken place in Texas alone. We took a closer look at the practice that is taking so many lives by examining the number of executions over time, analyzing the number of executions per state, and exploring the inmates' mindset in their final moments.
